About the studio

When my grandfather handed over his old workshop in Rodgau, Germany to me in 2015, there was only one conceivable option for me: to build a recording studio. But how do you start something like that? Fortunately, I was able to convince my father of the idea because without him, the studio wouldn't be finished today. After a year of planning, we started with the recording room, completely decoupled in a room-in-room construction, without parallel walls and with a soundproof glass door. After that, we began the expansion of the control room. My father took care of the drywall and laid the floor, my grandfather plastered and painted the walls, my uncle helped with the door, Felix built a table for my Soundtracs console, and I installed the electrical wiring and soldered 800 connectors by hand...

After 5 challenging years, we unfortunately finished just in time for the lockdown, and the studio remained mostly inactive except for a few small productions. I thought about how I could still use the space and started giving music lessons.

Since 2022, I can finally offer my full range of services. From recording to editing to mixing. From demos to podcasts to major productions. From singer-songwriters to bands. Even live recordings are possible in the Broken Vibe Studios in Rodgau.

About the lessons

I've been making music since the age of 6. Starting with trumpet and keyboard, I moved on to bass, shortly after to the guitar, and my first band. Later, I took up the drums and had my first significant performances. Over the last 20 years, I've played countless gigs in various formations, both nationally and internationally, and continue to do so today.

I primarily offer lessons in guitar (acoustic & electric) and bass for beginners and advanced players. Occasionally, upon request, I also provide lessons for drums and piano.

Additionally, I offer consultation sessions in the areas of audio, home recording, and band coaching.
